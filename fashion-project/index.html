Fashion trends are ever-evolving, reflecting the dynamic nature of culture, technology, and personal expression. In recent times, there's been a shift towards sustainable fashion, vintage revivals, and bold, experimental styles. Oversized silhouettes, vibrant colors, and gender-neutral clothing are gaining popularity among the youth. Social media and influencers play a major role in setting trends, making fashion more accessible and fast-changing than ever before. Comfort and individuality are now at the heart of modern fashion choices, as people embrace unique styles that reflect their personality rather than blindly following the crowd.
